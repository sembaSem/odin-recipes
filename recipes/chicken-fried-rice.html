Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A stir fry with chicken, rice, soy sauce and veggies like peas, carrots, celery and bell peppers. Yields 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 ½ pounds cooked, cubed chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 2 selery stalks, chopped
  • 1 large red bellpepper, diced
  • ¾ cup fresh pea pods, halved
  • ½ large green bellpepper, diced
  • 6 cups of cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and saute until soft, then add chicken and 2 tablespoons soy sauce and stir-fry for 5 to 6 minute
  2. Stir in carrots, celery, red bell pepper, pea pods and green bell pepper and stir-fry another 5 minutes. Then add rice and stir thoroughly.
  3. Finally, stir in scrambled eggs and 1/3 cup soy sauce, heat through and serve hot.
